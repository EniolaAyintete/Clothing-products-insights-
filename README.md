Clothing Product Insights Dashboard – Sales & Product Analysis
I recently designed and developed an end-to-end Power BI dashboard that provides clear insights into sales performance, product trends, retailer performance, and return patterns within a clothing retail business.


This dashboard was built using: Power BI, DAX for custom measures, Data modelling (star schema), UI/UX dashboard design principles, Data cleaning & transformation in Power Query
Sales Overview Dashboard 
This page focuses on understanding overall business performance at a glance.
Key Insights Included  Sales Count – Total number of orders placed. 
Total Profit – Overall profitability across all retail channels. 
Profit by Retailer Channel – Identifies the most profitable sales channel (Franchise, Local store, Supermarket, Small chain store).
 Sales Amount Quarterly (By Gender) – Breaks down purchasing behaviour across male and female customers. 
Annual Sales Amount Trend – Shows year-over-year performance and seasonality.
Impact & Why It Matters Managers can quickly identify which channels drive the most revenue, how customer segments perform, and whether the business is growing over time.

 Product Analysis Dashboard
 This page dives deeper into product-level insights and return patterns.
Key Insights Included
 Top Product by Profit – Hyperion Elements Jacket generated the highest profit. 
Total Units Sold & Returns – High-level KPIs that show product demand and customer satisfaction. 
Profit by Product Category – Helps identify categories that drive the highest profit (e.g., Hoodies & Sweatshirts). 
Total Returns by Product Name – Highlights products with high return rates for quality review. 
Customer Distribution by Gender – Shows which gender group interacts more with each product type.
Impact & Why It Matters This page helps the business optimize inventory, reduce returns, improve product quality, and understand customer purchasing patterns.
